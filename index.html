Resume Writing Guide

Best practices for crafting a standout resume.

1. Keep it to 1 page — Recruiters spend 6-7 seconds on initial scans.

2. Lead with impact — Use "Increased X by Y%" not "Responsible for X."

3. Tailor to the job — Mirror keywords from the job description.

4. Quantify everything — Numbers beat adjectives every time.

5. Use clean formatting — No tables, no graphics, no fancy fonts for ATS.

6. Include a summary — 2-3 lines that frame your narrative.

7. Proofread twice — Typos are the #1 reason resumes get rejected.
